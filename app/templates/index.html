Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Shamba Salama's technology and services.

What is Shamba Salama and how does it benefit smallholder farmers?

Shamba Salama is an innovative agricultural platform designed to empower smallholder farmers by providing them with timely, data-driven insights and essential farming resources. The platform addresses key challenges that small-scale farmers face, such as lack of access to extension services, unpredictable weather conditions, pest infestations, and difficulties in reaching markets.

Is Shamba Salama available offline?

Yes, Shamba Salama offers offline functionality to ensure farmers in rural areas with limited internet access can still use the platform. Once data is synced online, updates and analytics are seamlessly integrated.

What kind of support does Shamba Salama offer?

We offer ongoing support through local agricultural extension workers and provide access to expert consultants. Our team is available 24/7 to help with setup, troubleshooting, and training on how to best use our tools and technologies.
