Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to the settings page and enter your current password, then provide your new password and confirm it. Your password will be updated once submitted.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to the settings page and click "Delete Account". Be aware that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How can I view people who booked me?

To view bookings, go to the 'Bookings' section from your profile. You will see a list of people who have booked sessions with you, including their details and scheduled times.

How can I check messages from users?

To check your messages, go to the 'Bookings' section in your dashboard. There, you can view all messages sent to you and respond accordingly.

How do I participate in the forum?

To participate in the forum, go to the 'Forum' section, create an account, and start interacting with others by posting questions, answering, or joining discussions.

How can I access the settings?

To access your account settings, click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select 'Settings' from the dropdown menu. Here, you can update your profile, change your password, or delete your account.

How do I log out?

To log out, click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select 'Logout' from the dropdown menu. This will log you out of your account securely.

Contact Support

If you can't find the answer to your question or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to us!
