Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I change my password?

To change your password, go to the settings page and enter your current password, then provide your new password and confirm it. Your password will be updated once submitted.

How can I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to the settings page and click "Delete Account". Be aware that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How can I access the marketplace?

To access the marketplace, simply go to the 'Market' section from the main navigation menu. You'll be able to browse products, services, and make purchases directly from the platform.

How do I participate in the forum?

To participate in the forum, go to the 'Forum' section, create an account, and start interacting with others by posting questions, answering, or joining discussions.

How can I book an expert?

To book an expert, navigate to the 'Experts' page and browse the list of available professionals. Select an expert based on their expertise and availability, and follow the steps to schedule a session.

Contact Support

If you can't find the answer to your question or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to us!
